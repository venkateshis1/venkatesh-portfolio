About Me

Hi, I’m Venkatesh — Graphic Designer & Video Editor. With over 5 years of experience, I bring ideas to life through bold visuals and compelling edits.

Whether it’s designing a sleek logo, crafting a brand story, or editing a video that hits just the right emotional notes — I’m all about creating work that not only looks good but feels right.

I’m fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite and Canva, and I love turning abstract thoughts into visuals that speak clearly, connect instantly, and stay memorable.

When I’m not pushing pixels, you’ll find me exploring design trends, obsessing over color palettes, or just geeking out over great typography.
